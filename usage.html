Usage Guidance

You can find out how to use this self-learning module in this page.
'Self-learning Module - Logic':
Clicking on it will redirect you to the starting page, which houses all the exercises within the logic topic.
From there, you can navigate to any specific exercise by clicking the corresponding icon.

'Hint':
When you encounter a situation where you cannot proceed or become stuck, accessing hints with your suitable conditions allows you to move forward.

'Check':
It's used to check whether you get the idea and results right after reading the corresponding hint.

'Full Solution':
You can find the full solution for the specific exericse by clicking it.

What's the goal/meaning of this self-learning module?
By engaging with this module, you'll enhance your understanding of Natural Deduction through independent thinking and practical application.
The design of this module hopes that you can think and try by yourself, and get the answer directly to the least extent, which is the least helpful.

Have your final answer?
You can check it by clicking the 'Full Solution' button.

Get stucked?
It's highly advisable to begin by reading the corresponding hint and then attempt to move on independently.
Viewing the answer for the hint(i.e., click the 'check' button) should be considered as the last step.
